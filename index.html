🏕 Hidden lodging (alternate plays)

  • Sleeping Giant PP rustic cabins (Mon alternate) — Ontario Parks, often 7-night min in summer, call
  • Rossport Inn cabins — prettiest stretch of Hwy 17 (Tue night between TB and SSM if you split differently)
  • Killarney Mountain Lodge — 60-min detour off Hwy 17 Sudbury area, the curveball Canada Day play
  • Voyageur's Lodge Batchawana Bay — between SSM and Wawa, lakefront cabin vibe

Default plan: Holiday Inn Express TB + Holiday Inn Express SSM. Verified clean, free breakfast, on-highway. Boring but right.
